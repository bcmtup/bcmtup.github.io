Is Force-1 safe?

The Functioning of Force-1 is fully blockchain-protected. Participant leaves no personal data and has only his cryptocurrency wallet address connected via WEB 3.0 technology as a login. The entire Force-1 infrastructure is built on the operation of tamper-proof smart contracts, and the website is simply a projection of data from the blockchain. It makes no sense to hack into the website.

How much does it cost to participate in Force-1 USDT?

Participation in Force-1 USDT is the automatic activation of Metaverse levels in two marketing programs. Activation costs start at 5 USDT, not including minimum fees from the Smart Chain (BNB Chain) blockchain in the BNB cryptocurrency.

What do I need to get started in Force-1 USDT?

No special knowledge is required. Every participant must have their own smartphone or laptop with an installed app (Trust Wallet, TokenPocket or MetaMask), which has a personal Smart Chain (BNB Chain) cryptocurrency wallet set up. To register, you need to have at least a minimum amount - 5 USDT, and some BNB for a blockchain fee.
